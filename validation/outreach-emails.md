## 1. Primary cold email — title / operations manager

**To:** ALTA directory, state association lists, agency website contact  
**Target role:** Title manager, operations manager, agency owner  
**Channel:** Email

**Subject options (pick one):**
- 30-min research call — commercial exam workflow on SoftPro
- Quick question — how your agency handles overflow commercial files
- Industry research — SoftPro commercial examination (not a sales pitch)

**Body:**

```
Hi [First name],

I'm doing structured research on how independent title agencies handle complex commercial examination on SoftPro — long chains, entity vesting, multi-schedule commitments — before building anything in this space.

I'm not selling software or pitching a vendor today. I'm looking for 30 minutes with someone who owns commercial workflow (title manager, ops lead, or senior examiner) to understand:
- when files get outsourced vs kept in-house
- what works and doesn't with current bureaus/LPOs
- how exam output lands back in SoftPro today

We've completed desk research (ALTA complexity data, SoftPro 360/DataTrace workflows, bureau models) and are now validating with operators. Happy to share a short summary of aggregated findings with participants — no agency names disclosed.

Would a 30-minute call work the week of [date range]? I'm flexible on [Tue/Wed/Thu] mornings or early afternoons [timezone].

If you're not the right person, I'd appreciate a forward to whoever handles commercial exam or vendor relationships.

Thanks,
[Your name]
[Title — e.g. Founder / Product research]
[Email] · [Phone optional]
```
