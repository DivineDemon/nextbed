Final wedge: AI-native complex-file title search & examination as a managed service for independent agencies on non-Qualia production systems (SoftPro, RamQuest, legacy). Outcome-priced per complex file (~$100–300). Human examiner at agency approves. Runs alongside existing TPS.

Research Methodology

Actors: Founder (zero industry exposure, strong technical build ability) + AI research partner.

  • Global market analysis — including non-Western incumbents (the “Nected rule”)
  • Data-backed decisions only — no interviews, no whims
  • Primary filter: low funding density + no global incumbent on the wedge
  • Outsider-founder fit: tiebreaker only, not primary filter
  • Target: global, English-speaking B2B

Primary Metric: Funding Density Index (FDI)

FDI = (Total documented category VC, ~10 years) ÷ (Annual service market size) × 100

Lower FDI = more service dollars per venture dollar = less contested.

Product Spec (Drafted)

Versioned deterministic rules engine: (1) AI-assisted rule extraction, human-confirmed; (2) conflict detection across docs; (3) deterministic runtime lookup — no LLM in answer path; (4) versioning and audit trail. Target: 15–200 person companies, self-serve, undercut Glean's ~$60K/yr sales-led minimum.
