Phase 1 — Workflow Map Complete

End-to-end map of a commercial complex file on SoftPro — from order intake through commitment issuance. Sources: ALTA 2024/2026 complexity studies, ALTA residential closing flowchart, SoftPro commercial/automation docs, commercial examiner job descriptions, offshore bureau workflow models.

Key finding: Our managed bureau wedge sits in Search → Examination → Commitment draft — ALTA Segment 1 plus the typing/drafting work that precedes underwriter review.
